# Code of Conduct

We want ThisM to be a welcoming and respectful project for contributors and users.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but listen when impact is explained.
- Focus feedback on the work, not the person.
- Help keep discussions technical, clear, and inclusive.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or abusive language
- Personal attacks or discriminatory remarks
- Deliberate intimidation, trolling, or repeated disruption
- Sharing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or limit participation when behavior harms the project or its community.

## Reporting

If you need to report a problem, contact the repository maintainer privately through the contact method listed on the repository owner or organization profile instead of opening a public argument thread.
